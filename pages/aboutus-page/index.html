About Us

Who We are? What We do?

Goals and objectives of the Foundation Assanabil – is the construction of social facilities such as schools and health centers, besides the well, Submersible pumps and wells (rocking). Since 2004 BF Assanabil throughout Kyrgyzstan built around 24 schools all amenities. Wells were drilled about 700 have more than 500 deep-well pumps remote and inaccessible areas of Kyrgyzstan.

In addition to this basic duties BF Assanabil believes guardianship of orphans, currently over 3000 orphans are under the moral and material support fund.
